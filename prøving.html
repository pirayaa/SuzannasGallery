PROGRESSION


These paintings are the once she has dedicated the year to. As you can see they follow a certain city based theme. Some of them more abstract then the others. In addition to her previous work, where she mainly painted nature, this style reflects a certain melancholy. While her previous work was more a reflection of something old and beautiful. Her new work reflects something new and if it is beautiful is yet to be decided.

Artists note:


I hope my work is able to wake up some sort of emotion in the people watching them. In regards to my newest pieces i hope the people who sees them will think about how it is to walk in the city. Something that can be hard while being in a city because then the feeling can be undescribable and overwhelming. I hope these paintings will help people seeing the city from a different point of view
